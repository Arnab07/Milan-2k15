About MILAN

MILAN is a spectacular National level cultural competition where best talents from colleges across India converge and compete. Started in year 2008, Milan has graduated to its 8th edition with ebullient, innovative and creative Social. About 10,000 college students from all over India are expected to turn up for the Social. Milan has 45 exciting Social and a professional show. This four-day cultural fest is an opportunity for young college students to socialize and also showcase their hidden talents. It has over thirty Social that is scheduled to happen during the four days, and has participants competing in Dance, Music, Dramatics, Literature, Fashion and Creative Arts. Prominent celebrities like Kamal Hasan, Asha Bhosle, Vikram, Mammootty, Sidharth, Jeeva, Shriya Saran, Sharath Kumar, have graced the fest with their presence. Around 75 colleges from the southern states participate very enthusiastically along with many more colleges from other parts of the country.

Butterflies

The extravagant event for school students in the Kanchipuram district did, by all means, adhere to its motto “Inspiring Change.” Flocked by tiny tots of classes VII, VIII and IX from neighbouring schools SRM University was beaming with enthusiasm. The students stepped on to the platform to showcase their talents in the events organised by the University.
Over 70 enthusiastic students participated in each of the events, with a total of 740 students from 19 schools. The event kicked off with the track competition, and the students showcased their sporting abilities in the 100m, 4x100m relay, Sack race, and Lemon and Spoon race.
R. Sivakumar, a PT teacher at the Government Higher Secondary School of Kandigai said, “It is a welcoming initiative that a private university as big as SRM hosts events for school students, especially government and government aided schools. It shall motivate the students to take part in sporting events.”
The track and field winners were awarded medals, while each of the participating schools, and the winning school were given a shield as a token of appreciation. After the tracks were set on fire, events of literary exhibition commenced. Participants in the ‘Elocution’ and ‘Essay Writing’ events worked their hearts out, justifying the topics given to them.
The show concluded with the culturals events like Group Dance and Variety Show in Dr.T.P. Ganesan Auditorium. Honoured by the gracious presence of South Indian actor “Delhi Ganesan,” the Group Dance began. The judges of the Variety Show were Francis Raja and Dr. Shanti Pappu.
The children at this budding age possess great enthusiasm and energy. Showcasing talents such as mimicry, gymnastics, juggling, singing, and folk dances, audience members were spellbound. For their hard work, winners were awarded with gold, silver and bronze medal and were presented with certificates.
